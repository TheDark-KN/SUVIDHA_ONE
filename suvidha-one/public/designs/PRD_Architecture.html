# Suvidha One: Production-Grade Frontend UI Strategy & Architecture ## Phase 1: Product Understanding **User Personas:** - **The Elderly Citizen (e.g., Mr. Sharma):** Needs high contrast, large text, and voice guidance. Low tech-literacy. - **The Busy Worker (e.g., Priya):** Needs speed and efficiency. Mobile-first mindset, even at a kiosk. - **The Rural Visitor:** Needs multilingual support (Devanagari/Regional scripts) and visual cues for low literacy. **Core Use Cases:** - Utility Bill Payments (Electricity, Water, Gas). - Grievance Filing and Tracking. - Certificate Application (Birth, Death, Caste). - Municipal Service Directory Access. **Context of Usage:** - High-traffic public kiosks (32" - 55" touchscreens). - Varying lighting conditions (outdoors/indoors). --- ## Phase 2: UX Strategy & Principles - **Clear Hierarchy:** Bold headings and primary CTAs in Saffron (#FF6600). - **Minimum Cognitive Load:** One primary action per screen. Progressive disclosure for complex forms. - **Predictable Navigation:** Persistent bottom nav for Home, History, Help, and Settings. - **Error Prevention:** Validation on input, confirmation modals for destructive actions. - **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant. High contrast toggle, font scaling (+50%), and voice prompts. --- ## Phase 3: Design System (Bharat Sentinel - Refined) **Colors:** - Primary: #1A3C8F (Blue - Trust) - Accent: #FF6600 (Saffron - Action) - Success: #217346 (Green) - Error: #C0392B (Red) - Background: #FFFFFF (Surface), #F5F5F5 (Muted) **Typography (Noto Sans):** - Hero: 72-96px (Bold) - Page Heading: 60-72px (Bold) - Body: 48-56px (Regular) - Button: 56-72px (Medium) **Spacing:** 4px, 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px. --- ## Phase 4: Component Architecture (React/Tailwind) **Reusable UI Components:** - `Button`: Primary (Saffron), Secondary (Outlined Blue), Ghost. - `Input`: Large text inputs with integrated labels. - `Card`: Service tiles, Bill summaries, Contact info. - `TopNavBar`: Title, Language Toggle, Accessibility Toggle. - `BottomNavBar`: Navigation icons with bilingual labels. - `Modal`: Alerts, Confirmations, Loading states. - `AccessibilityPanel`: High Contrast and Font Scaling controls. --- ## Phase 5: Screen Architecture & Flow 1. **Welcome/Landing**: Visual invitation to touch. 2. **Language Selection**: 4x2 grid, high visual clarity. 3. **Authentication**: OTP/Aadhaar/Guest flows. 4. **Dashboard**: Service discovery via grid and refined search. 5. **Bill Payment**: Selection, Cart, and Checkout flow. 6. **Payment Processing**: Loading/Progress state. 7. **Success/Receipt**: Confirmation and multi-channel receipt sharing. 8. **Grievance**: Filing (form) and Tracking (list/detail). 9. **Help/Assistance**: FAQs and Physical help trigger. 10. **Error/Timeout**: Graceful recovery and security. --- ## Phase 6-10: Implementation & Performance - **Microinteractions**: Scale feedback on touch (scale-95), smooth page transitions. - **Frontend Architecture**: Next.js App Router, TailwindCSS, Headless UI for accessible components. - **Performance**: Image optimization, lazy loading for off-screen components, skeleton loaders. - **Offline Fallback**: Service workers for caching static assets and basic UI states.
